AI is powerful, but it’s only as good as the information you give it. If you’ve ever found yourself **re-explaining your business** every time you chat with an AI tool, you need a **Company Primer**, a structured way to provide context so AI can give you **more relevant, useful, and personalised** responses.

**What Is a Company Primer for AI?**

A Company Primer is a **pre-filled document** that outlines key details about your business. Instead of feeding AI scattered bits of information every time, you can **copy and paste your primer** at the start of a conversation to get instant, high-quality responses tailored to your needs.

Think of it as your **business cheat sheet for AI**. Once it understands who you are, what you do, and your goals, it can generate better insights, automate tasks, and even act as a strategic assistant.

**Why AI Needs Context**

AI works best when it has **clear, structured input**. Without context, it gives generic or vague answers. With a **Company Primer**, you can:

✅ **Get better marketing and content suggestions** (tailored to your industry and audience)

✅ **Speed up automation** (AI can write emails, reports, and strategies in your tone)

✅ **Improve brainstorming sessions** (AI can generate ideas based on your goals and challenges)

✅ **Enhance problem-solving** (AI can suggest solutions specific to your business model)

**How to Use the Company Primer**

1\. **Fill out the Prompt template** with details about your business, including your industry, services, audience, and goals.

2\. **Copy and paste the Populated version** at the start of any AI chat.

3\. **Ask AI-specific questions**, and it will now respond with tailored, relevant answers.

**Example Use Cases**

🔹 **AI as a Business Consultant:** “Based on my primer, what are three ways I can improve my customer acquisition strategy?”

🔹 **AI as a Marketing Assistant:** “Write a LinkedIn post in my brand voice about our latest achievement.”

🔹 **AI as a Content Creator:** “Draft a blog post explaining \[topic\] for my target audience.”

🔹 **AI as an Operations Helper:** “Suggest automations I can implement based on my services and workflow.”

**The Prompt Primer**

Below, you’ll find a **structured Prompt** that gathers all the necessary information. Once you fill it out, the **Populated** version becomes your AI-ready business profile.

```
Our business, [COMPANY NAME], is a [BUSINESS TYPE (e.g., B2B, B2C, SaaS)] company operating in the [INDUSTRY/SECTOR] since [YEAR FOUNDED]. We specialise in [CORE PRODUCTS/SERVICES] and primarily serve [TARGET AUDIENCE/CUSTOMER PROFILE]. Key information: 1. 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. 7. 8. 9. Unique Selling Proposition: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE WHAT SETS YOU APART] Main competitors: [LIST 2-3 PRIMARY COMPETITORS] Current marketing channels: [LIST YOUR ACTIVE MARKETING CHANNELS] Business goals for the next 12 months: [LIST 2-3 PRIMARY BUSINESS OBJECTIVES] Key challenges: [MENTION 1-2 SIGNIFICANT CHALLENGES FACING YOUR BUSINESS] Brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND’S TONE AND STYLE, e.g., PROFESSIONAL, FRIENDLY, INNOVATIVE] Target location(s): [SPECIFY IF YOU’RE LOCAL, NATIONAL, OR INTERNATIONAL] Company size: [NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES] Recent achievements or milestones: [MENTION ANY RECENT SIGNIFICANT DEVELOPMENTS]
```

Paste it into any AI tool, and you’ll get **smarter, more relevant responses**, no more repetitive explanations, no more generic advice. If you have ChatGPT you can add it to your Settings > Personalization > Custom Instructions and you won't need to reenter it.

AI is here to **work for you**, but only if you give it the right tools. This primer makes AI more useful, saves you time, and ensures you get **tailored, strategic insights** every time you chat.
